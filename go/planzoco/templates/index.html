planzoco

Every question finds its best answer.

Group decisions made over chat get lost: the question, the suggestions, and the votes end up scattered across dozens of messages, and by the time you need an answer nobody can reconstruct what the group actually agreed on.

Planzoco fixes that with one link per event. Add every question your group needs to settle, whether that's where you're going, what you're doing, or how you're getting there, and everyone can suggest options and vote on all of them from that single link.

No accounts, no sign-up, nothing to install. Just a name so votes count once, and you're in.
